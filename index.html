Credited work

The games listed below I have taken part of in one way or another. For some I wrote gameplay code, others were for level/environment design, and some were strictly for game art. I've been recognized as a "Game Changer" in Gamkedo Club for being among the most active in the group.

I've listed these titles according to the general scale of my implication, starting with the most impactful.

  • Blockological Society

    Move blocks to solve an array of puzzles.
